Developed by Experts

Developed by VIPKid’s Research Institute and perfected over 8 years, our
curriculum has been meticulously crafted based on in-depth research to
personalize learning. Our course structure is designed for holistic language

Why It Works

Our program combines full language immersion, repetition, and active learning.
Classes are tailored to each student’s individual learning level, based on a
comprehensive assessment test.
Our 1-on-1 flipped-classroom approach fosters creativity, confidence, and
critical thinking skills.

Class Preview

Before the live class, students watch a quick
video to preview class content. This boosts
understanding and confidence.

1-on-1 Live Class

During each 25-minute live class,
teachers combine interactive course
materials, real conversations,
songs, and games to keep students engaged.
Live instruction allows
for immediate teacher feedback.

Review

After class, students review materials to
reinforce learning through supplemental
worksheets, digital books, and videos.
